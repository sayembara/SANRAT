⚙️ Global Race Constants

Adjust the core physics, HP decay, and penalty rules for the entire SANRAT season. Changes take effect on the next "Daily Tick" on the Master Page.

🔋 HP Physics (Drain)

Controls how much HP Support cars lose per day while active.

Min: Max:

Formula: Random(Min-Max) - (Core / 20)

❤️ HP Physics (Healing)

Controls how much HP resting cars regain per day.

Min: Max:

Formula: Random(Min-Max) + (Core / 20)

⚖️ Standings Penalties (Round 2+)

Automatic adjustments applied during "Generate Season" based on previous round results.

Winner Penalty (Day 1 & 3):
  • If Propulsion 1-5 → Track: Straight. Else: Turn (Random).
  • If Traction 1-5 → Rain: 100%. Else: 0%.
  • If Stability 1-5 → Elevation: Uphill/Downhill. Else: Flatland.
Runner-up Penalty (Day 2):
  • Same logic as winner, applied specifically to Day 2 terrain and weather.
